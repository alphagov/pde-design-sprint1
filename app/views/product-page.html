Let users prove their eligibility online

The Personal Data Exchange network allows users to prove their eligibility for services online, so that you can be sure that you are providing services to people who qualify for them. Using Personal Data Exchange to prove peoples' eligibility means that you can build a fully digital, one-stop, self-serve, online service with a definitive answers for the citizen at the point of use.

Benefits of Personal Data Exchange

Making your department's data available through the Personal Data Exchange network gives you a single point of integration for exchanging citizen data with the rest of government. It is safe and secure. The Personal Data Exchange network provides a common language for the exchange of data which means that departments can spend more time negotiating the details for particular shares to ensure that they are necessary and proportionate and less time implementing and assuring a bespoke technical system for individual relationships.
