Get access to the Blue Badge eligibility checker

This tool will allow you to add an automatic check against the Personal Independence Payment (PIP) and Disability Living Allowance (DLA) from the Department of Work and Pensions. The check will tell you whether a citizen is automatically eligible for a Blue Badge according to the PIP and DLA data.

The get access to the checker, you will need to:

  • review and confirm how the eligibility check will be used
  • tell us who in your department will use the checker
  • accept the terms and conditions of the access agreement
