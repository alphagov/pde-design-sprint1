Manage access to your data

Use this service to give access to your department's data in a simple and secure way. Personal Data Exchange provides a technical and legal framework for government departments and bodies to query your data to check citizen eligibility.

Get started

Benefits of Personal Data Exchange

Departments using Personal Data Exchange can:

  • control and automate access to your data
  • set up legal agreements with service providers quickly and easily
  • minimise data transfer
  • integrate with a standardised eligibility checking system
  • meet user needs by providing seamless joined up services
  • comply with GDPR legislation on transparency and accountability
